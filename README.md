# Jetpack Safety
A simple mod that removes the damage players can receive when using actively the jetpack. It also removes the warning beeps, as they serve no purpose with this mod installed.
Now, it's time for your jetpack joyride.

## Installation
1. Install `BepInEx`
2. Drop 'BepInEx' **from the JetpackSafety.zip** into your Lethal Company folder.

## What it does
- Makes players unable to take damage or die when flying into obstacles.
- Prevents the jetpack from exploding
- As long as you are actively flying, you can hit the ground at any speed without dying or exploding
- Removes the emergency warning beeps

## What it doesn't do
- Does not make players invulnerable just for carrying a jetpack
- Does not prevent fall damage if the jetpack is not actively thrusting
- Does not affect damage from any other sources of damage

## Usage
No configuration needed.

> All players in the lobby are required to have the `Jetpack Safety` mod installed. Clients without the mod will receive an generic "an error occurred" message and be sent back to the main menu.

## Compatibility
Works for V81

This mod should work alongside most other mods.
